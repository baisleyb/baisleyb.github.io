and Me.

This is me. Morning coffee maker. Family tech support. Bathroom cleaner. I want to be able to do it all, but I know my limits. I can change a flat tire, but that is about all of my automotive abilities. I can replace a light switch or an outlet, but I won't attempt rewiring the house in fear it will burn down. And I can bench about 50 pounds. Come on, look at me, I'm not The Rock.

But I do think I am pretty good at telling computers what to do. Heck, I get paid to do it so someone else must agree. My job is improve the stability and reliability of enterprise financial applications. If clients can't trade with us, we lose money. I work to ensure that doesn't happen by finding bugs, automating manual tasks, writing synthetic checks, and resolving incidents before they become big problems. And in the little bit of spare time I may get, I like to create websites.
